Welcome

Hi, I’m Alex! I’m a Chicago burbs native and fertility nurse currently living in downtown Chicago with my high school sweetheart, Harry. We love to travel, and I can’t lie… my favorite part of experiencing a new city is eating my way through it! If you’re like us, you don’t travel to sit in a local McDs or touristy restaurant — you want to experience the best, local, must-try dishes a city has to offer.

It doesn’t take long to realize that eating out for every meal gets expensive, really fast. Plus, the drool-worthy restaurants you see on your favorite Food Network or Netflix shows usually come with a high price tag. Cue A Budget Abroad, your one stop shop for local, authentic food destinations that deliver amazing food and dining experiences without breaking your budget.

Another thing I love? A good deal. Harry will attest, I’m always looking for that sale, promo, or app that lets us eat for a fraction of the price… and that’s not easy to find when traveling abroad. Whether you’re traveling as a couple, with friends, or solo-dolo, A Budget Abroad helps you get a taste of the best food a city has to offer without worrying about affording your next hostel.

We serve it up hot and fast with three review categories:

Abroad All-Stars

are the spots where we loved what we ate and would recommend again and again. (And again!)

Splurge Worthy

are spots we’ve found with a slightly higher price tag, but well worth the price. (They’re a splurge, but not break the bank!)

Honorable Mentions

are additional spots we didn’t get to experience first-hand, but have garnered a reputation for great food at a great price. (Let us know what you think if you get a chance to try!)

You can call me cheap, you can call me frugal… or you can just call me A Budget Abroad – eat up & cheers!

Did I miss one of your favorites? Want to vet for an Honorable Mention? Disagree with one of the spots? Send me your recommendations and thoughts to alex@abudgetabroad.com.
